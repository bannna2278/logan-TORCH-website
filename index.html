Who we are

We are TORCH chasing. Our main goal is to chase torandoes and severe weather, collect data on them, and record to show to the public.

What we do

Our department is responsible for planning and executing chase missions. This involves coordinating with other departments, analyzing target data, and deploying chase teams to the field. We also handle the logistics of chase operations, including vehicle maintenance, equipment management, and team training.

Where to find us

The TORCH Chasing department is located at the main TORCH facility in East Central Florida. Our offices are situated on the second floor, in the west wing of the building.
